Critical Bottleneck Analysis

Kuwait Customs Clearance

Key Constraints:

  • Limited hours: 8am-1pm (Sat-Thu), Closed (Fri)
  • Only 30 operational hours per week vs. 168 hours for 24/7 facilities
  • Specialized inspection requirements for chemical materials
  • Accounts for 44.22% of total journey time (55.43 hours)

Efficiency Contrast:

  • Inbound clearance: 55.43 hours
  • Return clearance: 0.53 hours
  • Same facility, >100x efficiency difference
  • Demonstrates potential for dramatic improvement

Recommended Interventions

Strategic Engagement:

  • Engage Kuwait authorities to establish dedicated processing lanes
  • Negotiate extended hours or expedited processing for regular shipments
  • Leverage contrast with efficient return process to make business case

Operational Adjustments:

  • Implement robust scheduling to avoid Thursday/Friday arrivals
  • Optimize documentation preparation to minimize processing time
  • Explore pre-clearance options for regular shipments
